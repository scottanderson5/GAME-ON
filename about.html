WHO ARE WE?

The idea for GAMEon was born with two foundations in mind: discovery and opportunity.

Through discovery, gamers would have a chance to find and experience new board and card games they may have never heard of.

For game developers, they have the opportunity to market and receive feedback on their products.

PARTNERS

To make this idea work, we rely on the support from our partner companies

By offereing their games to be beta tested by real users, the companies provide gamers the chance of finding new, fun, games
